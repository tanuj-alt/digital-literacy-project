Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
1.Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media accounts.
3.Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank. (UPI Safety)
4.Always verify UPI collect requests before approving—remember, receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN. (UPI Safety)
5.Avoid clicking on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
6.Only download apps from official stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store).
7.Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi—avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts.
8.Double-check website URLs before entering login or payment details .
9.Regularly update your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities.
10.Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.
